AustroControl Feedback (based on my experience)

General Feeback

    Wide question bank available makes the exam relatively predictable, with often few to no new questions

    Certain subjects are more "banky" than others (Instruments and AirLaw are often 90%+ from the ATPLQ question bank directly, whereas subjects like Principles of flight, either you know it or you don't. The bank won't save you all the time)

    Generally, doing a few runs of the "Last 500 seen" question filter on ATPLQ (or similar) can be beneficial, especially the days before the exam. I did it for mine and it worked for Instruments and Air Law which were 90%+ from the bank word-for-word
